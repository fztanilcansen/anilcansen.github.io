What we offer

Custom

Custom Foot Orthotics

Built from your foot shape to improve comfort and loading for walking, work, and sport. Options for dress, athletic, and safety footwear.

  • 3D scan or foam box casting
  • Shell/material matched to your shoes
  • Break-in plan and fit follow-up
Medical-grade

Compression Stockings

Graduated compression (various mmHg) for swelling, venous support, and long-day comfort. Everyday, travel, and work styles.

  • Precise measurement & size fitting
  • Knee-high, thigh-high, pantyhose
  • Simple wear & care instructions
DonJoy

Knee Braces (DonJoy)

Stability for ACL, MCL/LCL, meniscus, or osteoarthritis. Off-the-shelf and custom options sized and tuned to your activity.

  • Activity-matched brace selection
  • Professional fitting & adjustments
  • Return-to-work/sport guidance

Who can benefit

Foot & Ankle

  • Plantar fasciitis & heel pain
  • Over-pronation/flat feet, high arches
  • Metatarsalgia, Achilles irritation

Knee

  • Osteoarthritis unloading
  • ACL/MCL/LCL support
  • Patellofemoral pain

Swelling & Standing

  • Leg fatigue on long shifts
  • Mild edema/venous support
  • Travel & prolonged sitting

Important: Devices work best with a short exercise plan and footwear tips. We keep it practical and easy.

How it works

1) Assessment
Foot/ankle/knee and gait review; footwear check; job/sport goals.
2) Casting/Measurement
3D scan or foam box for orthotics; precise limb measures for stockings/braces.
3) Fabrication & Fitting
Fit to your shoe/leg, comfort check, wear & care plan.
4) Follow-up
Fine-tuning after break-in; exercises to keep you moving comfortably.

What to expect

  • Orthotic break-in over 1–2 weeks
  • Compression wear schedule & skin checks
  • Knee brace don/doff & strap sequence
  • 2–4 simple exercises that support results
Insurance & Direct Billing

Many plans reimburse orthotics, compression, or bracing. Bring your policy; we’ll guide receipts/documentation. We offer Direct Billing where available.

FAQ

Do I need a doctor’s prescription?

You can be assessed without a prescription. Some plans require a physician prescription for reimbursement—check your policy.

How long do custom orthotics last?

Often 2–3 years with regular use. Footwear, body weight, and activity affect lifespan. We can assess wear annually.

Custom vs off-the-shelf—how do I choose?

Custom is built from your measurements for precise, targeted support. Off-the-shelf can suit some needs; we’ll recommend what fits your goals and budget.

Do you carry DonJoy knee braces?

Yes—sized and fitted in clinic. We match the model to your condition and activity (daily life, work, or sport).

Will I still need exercises?

Usually yes. Short, targeted strength/mobility helps comfort last. We keep programs simple and practical.
