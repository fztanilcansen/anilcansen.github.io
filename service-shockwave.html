Shockwave Therapy (Radial ESWT)

Non-invasive treatment for stubborn tendon and fascia pain. Sessions are short, intensity is adjustable, and we pair it with simple exercise so results last.

Book Now

What it is & why it helps

ESWT delivers focused acoustic pulses to the sore area. These pulses stimulate blood flow and cellular activity that support the body’s repair process and reduce pain signalling.

  • Drug-free and non-invasive
  • Targets chronic tendon/fascia problems
  • Great when other care hasn’t helped
  • Best results when combined with progressive loading (exercise)
What to expect:
  • During: firm tapping sensation; intensity adjusted to comfort
  • After: mild, temporary soreness (24–48h) can be normal
  • Plan: typically 3–5 sessions, about once per week

Common conditions we treat with ESWT

Foot & Ankle

  • Plantar fasciitis / heel spurs
  • Achilles tendinopathy
  • Shin splints

Knee & Hip

  • Patellar tendinopathy (jumper’s knee)
  • Gluteal tendinopathy, trochanteric bursitis
  • IT band irritation

Upper Limb & Shoulder

  • Tennis / golfer’s elbow
  • Rotator cuff pain
  • Calcific tendinopathy

Is ESWT right for me?

We assess first to confirm ESWT is appropriate and to build a simple exercise plan that locks in progress. ESWT is especially helpful for persistent symptoms > 3 months.

Ask a physiotherapist
Who should avoid it or get medical clearance
  • Pregnancy (avoid treatment areas)
  • Open wounds, infection, or tumors at the site
  • Pacemaker or implanted device near the site
  • Significant circulatory or nerve disorders at the site
  • Bleeding disorders / strong anticoagulants
  • Recent cortisone injection at the site (< 6 weeks)

FAQ

Does it hurt?

Most people feel firm tapping. We can adjust intensity at any time to keep you comfortable.

How many sessions will I need?

Typically 3–5 weekly sessions for most tendon/fascia problems. Chronic cases may need a few more.

When will I notice improvement?

Some feel change right away, most notice improvement over 2–4 weeks as tissue heals and you follow the exercise plan.

Is it covered?

Physiotherapy sessions may be reimbursed by extended health plans. We offer Direct Billing to many insurers.

Ready to get started?

Begin with a one-on-one assessment. If ESWT is a good fit, we’ll map a simple plan and track your progress.
