Direct Billing Services

Focus on your recovery, we'll handle the paperwork

Electronic Claims (e-Claims)

Yes—We offer direct billing to most major insurance providers. We can submit e-claims to 20+ major insurers, which collectively cover most privately insured Canadians. At your next visit, just ask us to bill your plan on your behalf—no extra paperwork for you.

How This Works With Private Insurance

Most extended health plans include physiotherapy benefits. With your consent, we'll submit the claim directly to your insurer and let you know if there's a co-pay or deductible due at the time of service.

Prefer to claim on your own? No problem—your appointment will be billed to you as physiotherapy, and we'll email you a detailed receipt you can submit to your insurer.

Questions & Answers

Do I need a doctor's referral before my assessment?

No. In Newfoundland and Labrador, physiotherapists are primary health care providers—you can book directly. Physiotherapists are self-regulated professionals with extensive university education.

Note: Some insurance plans still require a physician's referral for reimbursement. Please check your policy so you're not caught by surprise.
My referral is addressed to a different clinic. Can I come to you instead?

Yes. You're free to choose the clinic that best fits your needs. Referrals are used to support your insurance coverage—they do not lock you to a specific provider.

Specialized Billing Services

Motor Vehicle Accidents

If your injury is related to a motor vehicle accident, we'll work directly with your auto insurer to ensure your treatment is covered.

Workplace Injuries

We work with WSIB for injuries sustained at work. Let us know when scheduling and we'll handle the billing process.

Veterans Affairs

We are registered providers with Veterans Affairs Canada. Those with a K-number may be eligible for pre-approved treatment.
