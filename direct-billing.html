Direct Billing

Direct Billing (e-Claims) Yes—We offer direct billing. We can submit e-claims to 20+ major insurers, which collectively cover most privately insured Canadians. At your next visit, just ask us to bill your plan on your behalf—no extra paperwork for you. How This Works With Private Insurance Most extended health plans include physiotherapy benefits. With your consent, we’ll submit the claim directly to your insurer and let you know if there’s a co-pay or deductible due at the time of service. Prefer to claim on your own? No problem—your appointment will be billed to you as physiotherapy, and we’ll email you a detailed receipt you can submit to your insurer. Pelvic Health Physiotherapy is billed the same way as any other physiotherapy service. Questions & Answers Q: Do I need a doctor’s referral before my assessment? A: No. In Newfoundland & Labrador, physiotherapists are primary health care providers—you can book directly. Physiotherapists are self-regulated professionals with at least six years of university education and a Master’s degree in Physiotherapy. Note: Some insurance plans still require a physician’s referral for reimbursement. Please check your policy so you’re not caught by surprise. Q: My referral is addressed to a different clinic. Can I come to you instead? A: Yes. You’re free to choose the clinic that best fits your needs. Referrals are used to support your insurance coverage—they do not lock you to a specific provider. Q: What forms of private payment do you accept? A: Debit, cash, VISA, Mastercard, Apple Pay, and Google/Android Pay. Motor Vehicle Accidents (MVA) If your injury is related to a motor vehicle accident (MVA), we’ll work with your auto insurer to ensure the treatment is covered. Please inform us when booking your appointment so we can provide the necessary documentation and assist with claims processing. Workplace Injuries We work with WorkplaceNL for injuries sustained at work. If your injury is work-related, let us know when you schedule your appointment, and we’ll help with the billing process and necessary forms. Veterans Affairs Canada We are registered providers with Medavie Blue Cross / Veterans Affairs Canada. We offer direct billing for veterans; those with a K-number may be eligible for pre-approved treatment. Contact us and we’ll confirm your coverage details.
