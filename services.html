Our Services

Personalized assessment, treatment, and progressive loading.

Physiotherapy

One-on-one assessment and treatment to improve mobility, reduce pain, and restore function using manual therapy, exercise, and education.

Workplace NL

Workplace NL–approved physiotherapy for work-related injuries, including return-to-work planning and case manager communication.

Motor Vehicle Accidents

Rehabilitation programs for whiplash and soft-tissue injuries following car accidents to help you safely return to normal activities.

Veteran Affairs Canada

Dedicated physiotherapy services for veterans covered under VAC, with individualized plans and clear communication.

Advanced Sports Rehabilitation

Performance-focused rehab integrating strength, mobility, and movement retraining tailored to your sport and goals.

Acupuncture

Evidence-informed acupuncture for pain relief, muscle tension, and inflammation, provided by licensed physiotherapists.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on joint and soft-tissue techniques (e.g., Maitland, Mulligan, myofascial release) to improve range, reduce stiffness, and promote recovery.

Vestibular Rehab / Vertigo

Treatment for dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders (including BPPV), with gaze-stabilization and habituation programs.

Shockwave Therapy

Non-invasive modality for chronic tendon pain (e.g., plantar fasciitis, calcific tendinopathy) to stimulate healing.

K-laser Grade IV (coming soon)

High-power laser therapy for pain and tissue healing. Availability coming soon.

Custom Foot Orthotics

Custom orthotics to support alignment and reduce stress on the feet, knees, hips, and spine — for work, daily walking, or sport.

Concussion / Whiplash

Safe, graded rehab including vestibular and vision retraining to restore full function after head and neck injuries.

Neurological Rehab

Programs for stroke, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’s disease, and other neurological conditions focused on mobility and independence.

Kinesiology / Exercise Therapy

Progressive, goal-based exercise programs to build strength, balance, and endurance — from early rehab to return-to-activity.
