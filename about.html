About Physio Island

One-on-one physiotherapy. Clear plan. Real results.

We’re a Burin-based clinic built around private 1:1 care, precise diagnosis, and evidence-based treatment. Every visit is with a registered physiotherapist—no double-booking, no hand-offs—so your time turns into progress.

With 10+ years in outpatient orthopedics, sports rehab, and vestibular care, we combine hands-on therapy, targeted exercise, and clear education. We measure what matters using validated outcome tools, adjust quickly, and show your milestones as you recover.

Modern methods & technology

Structured vestibular assessment with VNG vestibular assessment glasses, shockwave therapy, and medical acupuncture when appropriate. Plans are refined using current research and guidelines. K-Laser Grade IV (coming soon) • Lumbar spinal decompression (coming soon).
