About Us

Evidence-based physiotherapy with one-on-one care, personalized treatment plans, and advanced rehabilitation programs tailored to each patient’s goals. We focus on restoring movement, improving strength, and preventing future injuries.

Anilcan Sen, PT

Registered Physiotherapist (#1011)

Jon (Anılcan) is a registered physiotherapist with 10+ years of experience in Turkey and Canada (Alberta & Newfoundland).

He helps patients with:

  • WorkplaceNL and motor vehicle accident (MVA) injuries
  • Neck, back and shoulder pain
  • Sports injuries
  • Vestibular & concussion problems
  • TMJ / jaw pain
  • Post-surgical rehab

He uses hands-on treatment (manual therapy, myofascial release, medical acupuncture) to reduce pain and restore movement.

Jon also explains the problem in simple language so patients know what to do at home and how to prevent it. His goal: not just to take the pain away, but to help you move confidently and get back to the activities you love.
